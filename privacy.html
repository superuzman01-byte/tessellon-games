Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Tessellon Games operates the Crystalloop mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Data Collection and Use

We believe in absolute user privacy. Crystalloop does not require you to create an account, and we do not collect, store, or harvest any personal data or identifying information while you play the game.

2. Data Handling

Because we do not gather any user information, no data is shared with third parties, advertisers, or external entities. Any gameplay progress is saved locally directly on your device storage.

3. Children's Privacy

Our application does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is fully compliant with standard child safety guidelines.
